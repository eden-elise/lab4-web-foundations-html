    Better Sleep Tips

    I am working on bettering my sleep schedule as it directly impacts productivity and mood, here is some things im trying to implement!

    • Same bedtime, same wake time. The human brain works well with routines.
    • Light matters: Morning light = “wake up”, dim lights 1–2h before bed.
    • Caffeine cutoff: None after 7 hours ~8 hours before bedtime (I struggle here).
    • Bed = sleep. If not asleep in 20 min, get up, read, then try again.
    • Phones down: PUT IT AWAY (blue light + doomscroll = BAD SLEEP️).
    • Melatonin(meh-LAH-toh-nin) is your friend—timing matters.

    Starting now: I will set Do Not Disturb nightly so alerts don’t wake me.

    Sleep Duration Goal: target 8 hours.

    Simple Bedtime Routine

    1. Dim lights and put phone away(5 min).
    2. Brush teeth, wash face(10 min).
    3. Set out clothes for tomorrow(5 min).
    4. Read 10–15 minutes (15 min), as my roomie Emma says I cannot read longer the 10 minutes without falling asleep
    5. Lights out - at a consistent time(5 min).
